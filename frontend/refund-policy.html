Refund Policy

Fair and transparent refund process with clear timelines and customer-focused terms

Refund Processing

We process refunds efficiently while maintaining quality standards:

  • Processing Time: 7-10 business days after inspection
  • Quality Check: Careful inspection of returned items
  • Original Payment: Refund to your original payment method
  • Confirmation: Email notification when processed

How Refunds Work

Our step-by-step refund process ensures transparency:

  • Step 1: We receive your return package
  • Step 2: Quality inspection (7-10 business days)
  • Step 3: Refund processing upon approval
  • Step 4: Funds returned to original payment method
  • Step 5: Additional 3-5 days for funds to appear

Payment Methods

Refunds are processed according to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Appears as statement credit
  • Digital Wallets: Direct credit to wallet account
  • Bank Transfers: Return to bank account on file
  • UPI Payments: Processed within 5-7 business days

Refund Status

Track your refund progress easily:

  • Account Dashboard: Check order history online
  • Email Updates: Automatic status notifications
  • Customer Support: Contact us with order number
  • Payment Statement: Monitor for credit posting

Manufacturing Defects

When genuine quality issues occur, we prioritize your satisfaction with expedited processing and comprehensive support.

Defect Resolution

For manufacturing defects, we provide full support:

  • Full Refund: Including original shipping charges
  • Free Return: We cover return shipping costs
  • Fast Processing: 3-5 business days priority handling
  • Replacement Option: Exchange for new item available
  • Immediate Contact: Report defects right away

Non-Refundable Items

Certain items have specific guidelines we follow:

  • Final Sale Items: Clearly marked clearance pieces
  • Return Window: Items outside 7-day return period (or 2-day exchange period)
  • Condition Requirements: Worn, altered, or damaged items
  • Original Packaging: Items missing tags or packaging
  • Special Cases: Contact us to discuss circumstances

Refund Processing Time

Once your return is received and approved, refunds are processed within the following timeframes:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days to appear on statement
  • Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days for refund posting
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days for processing
  • Store Credit: Immediate credit to your account
  • Holiday Periods: Additional 2-3 days during peak seasons

Need Refund Assistance?

Our customer service team is here to help with any refund questions or concerns.
