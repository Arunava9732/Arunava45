Shipping & Delivery

Reliable worldwide shipping with comprehensive tracking and customer support

Shipping Options

We offer worldwide shipping with multiple options to suit your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-10 business days worldwide
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days for faster delivery
  • Premium Shipping: 1-2 business days with priority handling
  • Free Shipping: Free shipping on all orders

Delivery Coverage

We deliver to addresses worldwide with reliable postal services:

  • India: All major cities and towns covered
  • International: 150+ countries supported
  • Remote Areas: Additional charges may apply
  • PO Boxes: Available for select locations

Order Tracking

Stay informed throughout your order journey:

  • Tracking Number: Provided via email after shipping
  • Real-time Updates: Track your package status online
  • Delivery Notifications: SMS and email alerts included
  • Customer Support: Available for tracking assistance

Shipping Protection

Your orders are protected during transit:

  • Secure Packaging: Premium materials for protection
  • Insurance Coverage: Automatic protection on all orders
  • Quality Checks: Each package inspected before shipping
  • Loss Protection: Full refund or replacement if lost

Delivery Concerns

While we strive for perfect delivery, occasional issues may arise. Our customer service team is here to help resolve any delivery concerns promptly and fairly.

Processing Time

Order processing begins once payment is confirmed:

  • Ready-to-Ship Items: 1-2 business days
  • Custom Orders: 3-5 business days
  • Made-to-Order: 7-14 business days
  • Holiday Periods: Additional processing time

International Shipping

Global delivery with customs and duty information:

  • Customs Clearance: We handle all necessary paperwork
  • Import Duties: Customer responsible for duties/taxes
  • Delivery Delays: Customs processing may add time
  • Documentation: Commercial invoice included

Shipping Insurance

Optional shipping insurance protects your valuable orders against loss, theft, or damage during transit.

  • Coverage up to full order value
  • Protection against loss and damage
  • Quick claims processing
  • Additional cost based on order value
  • Available for all shipping methods

Lost Package Protection

If your package is lost or significantly delayed, we work with carriers to locate it or provide resolution.

  • Investigation within 7 business days
  • Full refund or replacement offered
  • Extended search for valuable orders
  • Insurance claims filed automatically
  • Priority handling for lost packages

Need Shipping Assistance?

Our customer service team is available to help with any shipping questions or concerns.
