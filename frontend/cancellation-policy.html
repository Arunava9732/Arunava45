Cancellation Policy

We strive to provide flexibility while maintaining efficient production processes. Our cancellation policy balances customer needs with operational requirements.

24-Hour Cancellation Window

We offer a 24-hour cancellation window to provide flexibility. After this period, your order enters our production process and cannot be cancelled to ensure quality and efficiency.

  • Clock starts from order confirmation
  • Contact us immediately if needed
  • Full refund available within window
  • Processing begins after 24 hours

How to Cancel Your Order

To cancel your order, please follow these simple steps. We recommend contacting us as soon as possible for the best experience.

  • Note your order number from confirmation email
  • Contact our support team immediately
  • Provide order number and cancellation reason
  • Receive confirmation via email
  • Refund processed if within 24-hour window

Refund for Cancelled Orders

If your order is cancelled within the 24-hour window, you receive a full refund. This includes all charges to ensure complete satisfaction.

  • Full refund of product cost
  • Full refund of shipping charges
  • Processed within 3-5 business days
  • Funds returned to original payment method
  • No cancellation fees within window

Orders After 24 Hours

Once your order has entered production, it cannot be cancelled. We appreciate your understanding as we begin crafting your BLACKONN piece.

  • Order proceeds to production
  • Use return policy after delivery
  • Contact us for special circumstances
  • Quality assurance begins immediately

Cancellation Terms

Our terms are designed to be fair and transparent. We want you to be completely satisfied with your decision to purchase from BLACKONN.

  • Within 24 hours: Full refund including all charges
  • After 24 hours: Special circumstances reviewed
  • Reasonable processing fees may apply for late cancellations
  • Contact us to discuss your specific situation

Order Status Tracking

Stay informed throughout your order journey. We provide regular updates to keep you connected with your BLACKONN creation.

  • Email confirmation upon order placement
  • Production status updates
  • Shipping notification with tracking
  • Delivery confirmation
  • Regular status communications

Special Circumstances

We understand that situations arise. If you have extenuating circumstances for cancelling after 24 hours, please contact our support team immediately. We will review your case and determine if we can accommodate special requests.

Customer Support

Our customer service team is here to assist you. We aim to respond to all inquiries promptly during business hours.

  • Monday - Friday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM IST
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM IST
  • Email responses within 24 hours
  • Phone support for urgent issues
  • Dedicated cancellation support

Policy Updates

We may update this policy to better serve our customers. Any changes will be communicated clearly and applied fairly.

  • Advance notice for significant changes
  • Email notifications to registered customers
  • Website policy page updates
  • Existing orders honored under current terms

Our customer service team is here to assist you every step of the way.
