Cancellation Policy

We strive to provide flexibility while maintaining efficient production processes. Our cancellation policy balances customer needs with operational requirements.

Cancel Before Shipping

You can cancel your order anytime before it is shipped. Once shipped, the order cannot be cancelled. Cancellations before shipping are processed automatically with no admin approval required.

  • Cancel anytime before shipping
  • Automatic approval - no waiting
  • Stock is restored immediately
  • Full refund if paid online
  • Once shipped, cancellation not possible

How to Cancel Your Order

To cancel your order, simply go to your profile, find the order, and click the Cancel button. The process is instant for orders not yet shipped.

  • Go to your Profile â†’ My Orders
  • Find the order you want to cancel
  • Click the "Cancel" button
  • Select reason and confirm
  • Order cancelled instantly if not shipped

Refund for Cancelled Orders

If your order is cancelled before shipping and you paid online, you receive a full refund. COD orders have no charges to refund.

  • Full refund of product cost
  • Full refund of shipping charges
  • Processed within 5-7 business days
  • Funds returned to original payment method
  • No cancellation fees

Orders Already Shipped

Once your order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You can refuse delivery or use our return policy after receiving the order.

  • Shipped orders cannot be cancelled
  • You may refuse delivery
  • Use 7-day return policy after delivery
  • Use 2-day exchange policy if needed
  • Contact us for special circumstances

Cancellation Terms Summary

Our terms are designed to be fair and transparent. Here's a quick summary of our cancellation policy.

  • Before Shipping: Instant cancellation, full refund
  • After Shipping: Cannot be cancelled
  • Delivered Orders: Use 7-day return policy
  • Exchange: 2-day window after delivery
  • No cancellation fees for pre-shipment cancellations

Order Status Tracking

Stay informed throughout your order journey. We provide regular updates to keep you connected with your BLACKONN creation.

  • Email confirmation upon order placement
  • Production status updates
  • Shipping notification with tracking
  • Delivery confirmation
  • Regular status communications

Key Points

Cancel before shipping: Automatic approval, no waiting. After shipping: Order cannot be cancelled - use our return policy instead. Exchange: 2-day window after delivery with admin approval. Return: 7-day window after delivery.
