Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive sizing charts. When in doubt, size up for a relaxed, streetwear look.

How to Measure

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and snug but not tight.

Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to the bottom hem of the garment.

Shoulder

Measure from one shoulder edge to the other across the back of the garment.

Sleeve Length

Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve cuff.
