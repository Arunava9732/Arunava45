Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Your trust and security are our highest priorities.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

  • Industry-leading encryption protects your data
  • Transparent data practices you can trust
  • Your privacy rights are always respected
  • Secure handling of all personal information

Information We Collect

We collect only the essential information necessary to provide you with our services and enhance your experience.

  • Account details for personalized service
  • Shipping information for accurate delivery
  • Payment data processed through secure gateways
  • Order history to provide excellent support
  • Communication data to assist you effectively

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide you with the best possible service and experience.

  • Process and fulfill orders efficiently
  • Send important order updates and notifications
  • Provide personalized customer support
  • Improve our services based on your feedback
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access

Data Protection & Security

We are committed to protecting your personal information with industry-standard security measures.

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • PCI DSS compliance for payment security
  • Advanced password hashing and storage
  • Limited access to authorized staff only
  • Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Sharing

We share your information only when necessary to provide our services and with your best interests in mind.

  • Shipping partners for delivery services
  • Certified payment processors only
  • Trusted service providers for essential functions
  • Legal requirements when absolutely necessary
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Analytics to improve your experience
  • Full control over cookie preferences
  • Easy opt-out options available
  • No tracking across other websites

Your Rights & Choices

You have complete control over your personal information and can exercise your rights at any time. We make it easy to access, update, or delete your data.

Data Management

You have full control over your personal data with easy-to-use tools and processes.

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Update or correct any information
  • Request complete data deletion
  • Easy opt-out from marketing communications
  • Data portability in standard formats

International Data Transfers

If you access our website from outside India, your information may be transferred to and processed in India.

  • Data stored securely in India
  • GDPR compliance for European users
  • All transfers meet legal requirements
  • Your consent ensures compliant processing

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

  • Account data retained while account is active
  • Order history kept for 7 years for tax purposes
  • Communication records retained for 3 years
  • Marketing data deleted upon request or after 2 years of inactivity
  • Legal holds may extend retention periods

Children's Privacy

BLACKONN is committed to protecting children's privacy. Our services are not intended for children under 13.

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Parents can request deletion of child's data
  • COPPA compliance for U.S. users
  • Age verification for certain products
  • Educational content available for all ages

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

  • Email notifications for significant changes
  • Website notices for all updates
  • Clear summaries of key changes
  • Your continued use indicates acceptance

Privacy Support

Our privacy team is here to help with any questions or concerns about your personal information.

  • Dedicated privacy inquiry support
  • Response within 48 hours
  • Expert assistance for all privacy matters
  • Guidance on exercising your rights
  • Help with data management requests

Your privacy is important to us. Contact our privacy team for any questions or concerns.
